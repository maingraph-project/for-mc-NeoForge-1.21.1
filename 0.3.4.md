## v0.3.4：“全局与共享”更新

- **新增**:
    - **跨存档全局蓝图**: 支持将蓝图存储在全局目录中，使其在所有单机存档间通用。

- **修复**:
    - **映射泄露问题**: 修复了保存存档映射时会将全局映射错误地固化到存档文件中的 Bug，现在仅保存存档特有的增量映射。

    
## v0.3.4：Global & Shared Update

- **New**:
    - **Cross-save Global Blueprints**: Support for storing blueprints in a global directory, making them accessible across all single-player worlds.

- **Fix**:
    - **Mapping Leakage**: Fixed a bug where saving world mappings would incorrectly hardcode global mappings into the world file; it now only saves incremental mappings unique to the world.
